Welcome to the Natural Disaster Awareness Dashboard. This platform provides essential information to help you stay informed and prepared for natural disasters. Our mission is to promote disaster awareness and equip communities with the knowledge needed to respond effectively to emergencies.

The importance of disaster awareness cannot be overstated. Natural disasters can strike unexpectedly, causing significant loss of life and property. By staying informed through this dashboard, you can take proactive steps to protect yourself, your family, and your community.

To stay safe: Always have an emergency kit ready, know your evacuation routes, stay tuned to official alerts, and follow instructions from local authorities. Remember, preparation today saves lives tomorrow.

Emergency Contact

In case of emergency, contact these key personnel and organizations for immediate assistance.

National Emergency Services

Dial 911 for immediate emergency response.

FEMA Disaster Response

Call 1-800-621-3362 for federal disaster assistance and information.

American Red Cross

Contact 1-800-RED-CROSS (1-800-733-2767) for disaster relief and support.

Local Fire Department

Reach out to your local fire department for rescue and firefighting services.

Local Police Department

Contact local law enforcement for security and evacuation assistance.
